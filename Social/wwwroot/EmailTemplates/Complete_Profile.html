It only takes a minute:

You're always in control

 

 

Head to Settings and choose the gender, age and distance of people you're interested in meeting - and are happy to be seen by. Your Feed will display those nearest to you first.

Don't want people to know exactly where you are? Toggle on Personal Space and randomly offset your location within a 400m radius each time.

 

 

 

One last thing...

Before you go, turn on notifications so we can let you know when a new message or connection request lands. We won't be annoying, promise.
