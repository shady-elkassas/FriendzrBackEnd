Welcome to Friendzr

We're so happy to have you with us. To get you started, we're sharing our three top tips for an awesome Friendzr experience. Enjoy!
Make your profile stand out

Friendzrs with fully completed profiles tend to get more connection requests. Share a little about yourself, what you like to do and how you prefer to connect. Add a touch of your personality with a few lines in the 'About me' section.

If you decide to upload your profile picture (optional but recommended!), we will ask you to take a selfie to verify you are who you say you are. But don't worry; it only takes a second, and we will not store or use this picture anywhere else.
Set up my profile
Control how you connect

Choose the gender, age and the maximum distance of people you'd like to see - and are happy to be seen by. Your Feed will display those nearest to you first. Toggle on the interest switch at the top to see those with the highest interest match first. 

Don't want people to know exactly where you are? Toggle on Personal Space and randomly offset your location within a 400m radius each time.
Change my settings
Find fun things to do

Head to the Events section to see fun events near you. If it's a specific gig you're after (say, comedy) or a particular date, switch on the interest toggle at the top to narrow things down. 

You can also use Friendzr to set up your own events and share them with others in a group chat (no more annoying WhatsApp groups).
